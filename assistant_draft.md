# A new Introduction

I am a designer working at the intersection of creativity and industry. My work spans visual communication, spatial experiences, and human-centered design, with a focus on how design shapes the way people interact with the world around them.

I work in tech, and one of the problems I care most deeply about is fostering more empathy in the world. In an increasingly digital and fast-paced environment, I believe that how we design products, spaces, and experiences has the power to bring people closer together or push them further apart.
